A Music Flesh Map

In this unusual visualization, artists Fernanda Viégas and Martin Wattenberg have analyzed the lyrics of 10,000 songs looking for the words that describe body parts and have plotted the results as a function of 11 music genres. The resulting chart highlights the differences in lyric content between the various genres. It's an interesting graphic (but be warned, some parts of the graph - especially the Hip Hop genre - are not safe for work).
