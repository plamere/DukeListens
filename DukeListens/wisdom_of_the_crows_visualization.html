Wisdom of the crowds + visualization == Many Eyes

Many Eyes is an experiment in applying the wisdom of the crowds to data visualization.  If you have an interesting set of data, you can upload it to Many Eyes and use  various tools to create visualizations of this data.  The goal of Many Eyes is to encourage sharing and conversation around visualizations.  It's an interesting idea, with some interesting products so far:
