Yet another social music site - mog.com





Mog is a social music site, still in private beta.  It looks like it will be similar to  MySpace with a heavy music influence, as well as some automated recommender and user-to-user affinity matching. 

The social music discovery space is starting to get quite crowded.  Let's see how many we can name:
  • last.fm
  • MusicMobs
  • MusicStrands
  • Mercora
  • WebJay
  • ccMixterSoundFlavor
  • upTo11.net
  • plurn
  • GenieLab
  • Jamendo
  • Muiso
  • internet iTunes Registry
  • Gnoosic
  • SoundFlavor
  • Gnoosic
That's quite a list.  The social side of things is starting to get a bit crowded, but it seems that everyone of these services brings something unique to the table.
