Musicmobs joins the last.fm Social Music Revolution

Toby Padilla, the creator of Musicmobs, one of the first social music sites has joined the last.fm team.  Musicmobs was clearly ahead of the curve - pioneering many aspects that are now seen as requirements in the 'Music 2.0' space. Social playlisting, recommendation, open web services,  implicit gathering of taste data, - the 'hipster <-> mainstream slider', excellent design, cool name: Musicmobs has it all.  It will be sad to see Musicmobs close its doors as Toby moves to Last.fm, but I am really excited to see what happens when you connect the vision that Toby has for Music 2.0 with the resources that Last.fm has.   Congrats to Toby and kudos to Last.fm for building the social music dream team!
