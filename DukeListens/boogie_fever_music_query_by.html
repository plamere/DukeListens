Boogie Fever ... music query by body temperature

Jeremy Pickens points out this article in the new scientist describing a recent patent by Disney for selecting music based upon a users mood as determined by sensors that measure body temperature, perspiration and pulse rate. Interesting with the new Disney / Jobs connection. Perhaps we'll see an iPod with a mood-based shuffle play.
