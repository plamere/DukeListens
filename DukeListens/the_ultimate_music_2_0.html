The Ultimate Music 2.0 Directory

RocketSurgeon (aka Jadam Kahn) got tired of the wiki style of the original music 2.0 directory - it was confusing and prone to misuse (take a look at the current home page). So he decided to build his own Ultimate Music 2.0 directory.  Jadam has written descriptions of over 200 Music 2.0 companies (from AOL to ZuKool) in blog style.  The neat thing is that he has categorized/tagged each of the companies with tags like:

  • Ad Supported 
  • Artist 2.0
  • Business Services 
  • CDs 
  • College 
  • Community Content 
  • Concert 2.0 
  • Discovery 
  • Download Stores 
  • DRM-Free 
  • Editiorial 
  • Game Console 
  • Label 2.0 
  • Live Music
  • Lyrics 
  • Mashups 
  • Media Players 
  • Media Storage 
  • Metadata 
  • Mobile 
  • Music News 
  • Music Recognition 
  • Music Reference 
  • Music Video 
  • Personalization 
  • Playlisting
  • Plug-Ins 
  • Podcasting 
  • Radio 2.0
  • Remote Access 
  • Reviews 
  • Satellite 
  • Scrobbling 
  • Search 
  • Social Network 
  • Subscription Services 
  • Tools 
  • Trends
  • Visualizations 
  • Widgets
 

Read more about the new directory in Rocket Surgeons blog or visit the Ultimate Music 2.0 Directory.
