Search Inside the Music - a two page white paper

At the Recommenders Summer School in Bilbao Spain last week, I had the opportunity to demonstrate the Search Inside the Music project.  Search Inside the Music is a system that helps people find new music by finding music that 'sounds similar' to music that they already like.  I received quite a bit of positive feedback from people at the conference.  Many folks wanted to learn more about how the system works.  I have a two-pager that gives a general overview of how the system works.  You can read about it here:  Search Inside the Music - Two Pager.
