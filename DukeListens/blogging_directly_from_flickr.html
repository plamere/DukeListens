blogging directly from Flickr


Rivers
Originally uploaded by gavindow.
Last week, I configured things so I could blog directly from docs.google.com. This week, I configured flickr so I can blog to blogs.sun.com using Flickr's 'blog this photo' . Again the setup was very easy, and unlike the google posts, the flickr posts seem to have the proper timestamp.

Ah yes, and this is my favorite rock concert photo. It captures all that is Weezer. The untucked collared shirt, the sweater, the deferrential bow, with the rock salutes from the crowd. Nerd rock at its finest.
