Live radio on the iPhone

Live streaming radio is available for your iPhone.  Just point your iPhone (or iTouch) Safari browser at www.musicradio.com  This is still currently a prototype - the primary goal being to demonstrate that people are still interested in radio and devices like the iPhone are good candidates to serve up the traditional radio content.  Read more about this here: Live radio on the iPhone and the iPod touch. Right now I am listening to the traffic report for the M2 - via Classic FM Drivetime - (it is the end of the day rush hour in London). It's a bit surreal.
