Hey Paul, thanks for the post. To your scaling concerns about representing a full tag cloud this way, you're right in that would be impractical.

I would think of this more as a "top n" tags plus their correlations between each other (n probably < 10). I could imagine this existing with a tag cloud (not necessarily instead of it) with different tags from the cloud popping in and out with their analogous correlations.

But certainly if we consider Tufte's rules for visualizations as (paraphrased) "maximal information in minimal space"...this would probably fail.
