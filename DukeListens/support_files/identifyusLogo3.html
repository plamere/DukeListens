I regret to announce that http://iden.tify.us is over & done.

The website and logins are turned off and all embedded widget are deactivated.

While the site has been a great success in my eyes, outside influences has caused me to end the sites. I am writing up a post-mortem of the site and will post it on http://1821design.com when I am finished.

The idea of the site was somewhat simple, provide a place for people to help others identify music and for sometime the site was able to do this. There are many reasons that I am putting this site to rest, more to come later.

First off, I would like to personally thank every single user that participated. It has been a blast seeing what people's musical interests were and communicating with them.

I would like to thanks the sites that mentioned iden.tify.us or were even kind enough to write about us.

I would like to thank the visitors from the countless countries. It really is quite cool, when you build and run something that people across the world access and use.

Also, I am sorry if you have just recently signed up and were all excited about someone helping you out, hopefully someone still does, but it won't be here. :(

So long, and thanks for all the fish,
Tom Swift, your curious music curator
