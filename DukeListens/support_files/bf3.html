  • About Bjorn Fogelberg

    Welcome to my main place on the net. I'm a producer and remixer of electronic music - nowadays mainly music for the dance floor. I started out on the Commodore 64 in the mid 80s and soon moved on to synthesizers. To this date I have released two CD albums and a ton of singles and remixes. On Fogelberg.com you can always listen to my latest productions and read about interesting stuff. Enjoy your stay!
